Problem

Yoga studios run on instructors showing up and teaching classes. When something comes up an instructor can’t make it studios are on the hook. Finding substitutes can be a headache, especially last minute!

Solution

Yogi Flow is a simple solution of combining a scheduling and substitute system together. A way for studios to see all of their classes, their regular instructors and find new ones.

Objective

Design an easy to use app for Yoga studios scheduling and substitute needs. Currently there is a gap in the marketplace for apps that help yoga studios to find substitutes. Design for both iOS and Android

User Research

Research Goals

Understand the challenges yoga studio managers face with managaing their studio schedules.

Who is this app for?

Yoga studios who rely on more then one instructor to teach their classes.

WWhen will your audience use the app?

When a studio needs a substitute or when an instructor is looking to pick up more classes.

Where will they use the app?

On the go or at the studio and in any location.

What kind of tasks will users complete as they use the app?

Yoga studios will need to:

  • Create a studio profile with information on the classes offered and general vibe.
  • Schedule input to indicate what classes need to be filled, the ability to select groups or individuals, and then send notifications.
  • Search and browse substitute instructor profiles (certifications, experience, insurance coverage, and reviews)
  • Save schedule updates.
  • Save favorite instructors (create group favorites)
  • Send notifications.

    • *Yoga instructors will need to:

  • Create a profile with information on certifications, past experience, and insurance coverage.
  • Search studios and see opening in schedules.
  • Pick up classes (book sub offerings)
  • Receive notifications and messages.
  • View and save schedule updates.
  • Save and follow favorite studios.

    • *2nd design phase

    User Stories

    The app is designed to address the following user stories:

    “As a user, I want to see my studio(s) schedule and quickly see who’s teaching and where I still need substitutes.”

    “As a busy yoga studio owner with two studios I need to send out messages to multiple instructors and get my classes covered quickly, so I can focus on other things.”

    “As a boutique yoga studio manager I need to find instructors with specific qualifications”

    Usability Testing

    Using my mid-fidelity wireframes I created a prototype in Figma to test with potential users. I wanted to make sure that the overall flow made sense and there was no confusion in using the calendar, scheduling and requesting a substitute features. Number of Users: 6

    >

    Findings

  • Users wanted to have a key on the calendar/schedule screen so that the dots were easily understood by all users.
  • Some users were confused by verbiage used to request and accept substitute classes.
  • Users wanted to see a place where messages were easily created and sent.

    Conclusion

    I designed this app with studio owners/managers as the user. For full functionality this app would need to further be designed around instructors/substitutes use. The next phase of design would include features such as creating profiles, updating availability, messaging, saving favorite studios and potentially connecting with other instructors.
